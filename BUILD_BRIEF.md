## Verified Facts Kept

- Clinic has presented itself as a North London chiropractic clinic established in 2004.
- Location: Hampstead Chiropractic Clinic, Virgin Active, O2 Centre, 255 Finchley Road, London NW3 6LU.
- Phone: 020 7433 3323. Email shown by the official site is info@hampsteadchiropractic.co.uk.
- Official site says the clinic is near Finchley Road tube and Finchley Road and Frognal overground, with parking at the O2 Centre.
- Official site says most growth has come from referrals and word of mouth, appointments are not rushed, families are welcome, toys are available for children, and pillows are available for babies and expectant mothers.
- Official site says out-of-clinic-hours calls are diverted to a chiropractor.
- Official site says flexible hours include 10am-8pm Monday, Wednesday and Friday, and 8am-5pm Saturday. The previous unsourced daily 9am rows were replaced with this safer wording plus "call to confirm" for other days.
- Insurance pages say the clinic invoices private insurers directly, with GP referral sometimes required, and list major insurers including BUPA, AXA, Aviva, Cigna, Vitality Health, Simply Health, WPA, Allianz Worldwide Care and Standard Life.
- Prices retained from official price pages: initial consultation GBP120, chiropractic session GBP60, treatment under 16s GBP55, 5-session package GBP285, 10-session package GBP540.
- Practitioner copy is based on the clinic's own bio pages:
  - Nina van Dyk: Master's Degree in Chiropractic, Post Graduate Master's in Advanced Chiropractic Paediatrics, treats newborns to adults and expectant mothers, techniques include Diversified, Gonstead, Thompson, Myofascial Release and Acupuncture, current official bio lists General Chiropractic Council and United Chiropractic Association.
  - Samantha Allen: Welsh Institute of Chiropractic Master's, individualised treatment plans, diversified manipulation, soft tissue techniques and dry needling, registered with GCC and member of BCA.
  - Georgios Barmpouris: Welsh Institute of Chiropractic Master's, Postgraduate Licentiate training year with the GCC, interests include babies, children, families, headaches and migraines, techniques include Activator Protocol, Diversified, Myofascial Release and Medical Dry Needling.
- Testimonials used on the demo are from the clinic-controlled testimonials pages. Some quotations were shortened for layout without changing the represented meaning.

## Unsupported Claims Removed Or Softened

- "Est. by Nina van Dyk" was removed; the official pages support 2004 establishment and Nina as clinic director, but not the founding wording.
- "Three GCC-registered chiropractors" and "all members of the British Chiropractic Association" were removed; the current source pages list different registrations/memberships per practitioner.
- Exact daily opening rows previously showing 9am-7pm, 9am-8pm and 9am-6pm were replaced with the official site wording available in the source.
