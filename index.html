Why patients travel to see us

A calm, unhurried clinic families come back to

Since opening in 2004, most of our growth has come from patient referrals and word of mouth. We take the time to listen and adapt our approach to each individual — from your first phone call to your last check-up. Families are always welcome: we have toys to keep little ones happy, and special pillows for babies and expectant mothers, making treatment comfortable at every stage of life.

More about the clinic

Assessment

A thorough exam before any treatment begins

Every new patient receives an in-depth case history, postural and spinal analysis, and an orthopaedic and neurological examination. We use digital scanning technology alongside hands-on assessment to build a clear picture of your spine before your chiropractor explains their findings and agrees a treatment plan with you.

If your concerns fall outside what we can address in clinic, we have an established referral network to X-ray and MRI facilities, medical specialists, physiotherapists and podiatrists.
