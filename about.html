About the clinic

Providing high-quality chiropractic care in North London since 2004.

Our story

Founded on referrals and word of mouth

Since opening our doors in 2004, Hampstead Chiropractic Clinic has been committed to helping patients achieve better health and wellbeing through expert, personalised chiropractic care. We're proud that most of our growth has come from patient referrals and word of mouth — a reflection of the trust and satisfaction of those we treat.

Many of our loyal patients travel from far and wide to see us, valuing our honest advice, tailored treatments, and appointments that are never rushed. We take the time to listen and adapt our approach to each individual, always aiming for the best possible results.

A calm, family-friendly clinic

Designed to be a peaceful space

Our team is dedicated to creating a calm, caring and professional environment. From your first call, our friendly reception staff will do their best to offer flexible appointment times and answer your questions quickly and clearly.

We've designed our clinic to be a peaceful space, away from the noise of central London. Families are always welcome — we have toys to keep little ones happy, and special pillows for babies and expectant mothers, making treatment comfortable at every stage of life.
